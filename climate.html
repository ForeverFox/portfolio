Climate Change Series

Typography

This project is a typography series for my Typography course with MCAD in spring 2018. Students were meant to design pieces from the ground up, first using dummy copy, and then revise, remix, and rework, using an article with a theme of our choice. I chose the theme of climate change, specifically looking at what impact global warming might have on pathogens that have long been dormant in permafrost. The poster series laid the foundation for our final project: a 64-page electronic publication. For both of these projects, I selected two articles which focused on climate change in Greenland as a case study.

Technologies + Tools:

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
