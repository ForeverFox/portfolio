Fox & Owl Press

Website Redesign Project

This is a demo website that I prepared for Fox & Owl Press. I designed the original site and hand-drawn logo in 2012. I wanted to overhaul the previous website and create a fresh, modern look that was also mobile-friendly and responsive. I incorporated images and the Fox & Owl Press logo from the previous website to keep the original branding consistent, but more up to date. (Note: This site is a demo site, and remains unfinished.)

I hand-coded this site using HTML, CSS, Sass, JavaScript and jQuery.
