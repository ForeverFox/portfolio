Body of Work

Photography Project

This was a collabortion piece between myself and author Gili Estlin Hirsch. The author chose lines from her poems and had me write them on her body. I then took photographs which became a collection titled Body of Work.

Selections from this photography series were chosen for a bound paperback volume, which was offered as a reward for contributors to her Indiegogo campaign to raise funds for the production of Anna.
