TLV

Street Photography Book Project

When I visited Tel Aviv for the first time in 2012, I was struck by the incredible graffiti and street art adorning almost every building. I began to take photographs of the pieces that resonated most with me, and eventually decided to collect the images along with photographs of other scenes that I felt captured the character of Tel Aviv in a volume for print.

One-Off Print. 15.5cm x 15.5cm
