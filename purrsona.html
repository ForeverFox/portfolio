Purrsona App

UX Design Project

This was the final project for my UX / UI Design course with MCAD in fall 2017. Students were asked to design an app of their choosing, and perform in-depth UX research. I came up with the concept for an application that would help cat owners keep track of information about their cat(s)—e.g., medical records, feeding schedule, vaccinations, etc.—and also provide a way to share this information with cat-sitters. The app was also designed to allow owners and cat-sitters to communicate with one another online, so that owners could receive daily reports on the well-being of their cat(s).
